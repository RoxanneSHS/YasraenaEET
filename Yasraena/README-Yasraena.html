I. INSTALLATION

  1. To install the Yasraena Mod, first download the zip file. Open the zip file and extract all the files inside, which will be two (the .exe, and the folder containing everything else). Drop all these into your BGII - SoA folder. Then click the .exe and install Yasraena. That's it. TOB is required to play.

Though this mod is made using the WeiDU standard, and *should* be compatible with all other WeiDU-based mods, there is always the possibility for conflicts. We suggest you install this mod AFTER the official patches, Baldurdash, and TeamBG mods. If you encounter any problems installing the mod, please visit our forums.

II. UNINSTALLATION

Uninstalling is simple. Double-click the setup-yasraena.exe file and enter the given commands for Uninstallation.
Afterwards, you may delete the following files:

  • setup-yasraena.exe
  • setup-yasraena.tp2
  • setup-yasraena.debug
  • and the contents of the "yasraena" subfolder

V. GENERAL OVERVIEW

Yasraena begins at 9th level, appropriate for a party just leaving Chateau Irenicus. If you do not pick her up then, she can never be picked up. Also, if you ever drop her from the party, she will be gone for good.

Back to top

VI. SPOILERS

Yasraena gets on fairly well with all the Bioware NPCs. She has romance conflicts with Aerie, Viconia, and Jaheira. Even if all three Biowares are in the party, you will not see more than one conflict. (Viconia takes precedence.) It is dangerous to keep Viconia and Yasraena together in ToB.
